Frequently Asked Questions

What is the return policy?
– You have 30 days from date of delivery to return items for free for a full refund if the item is defective, damaged, or the incorrect product.
– Items must be sold and/or fulfilled by Amazon directly, otherwise the customer has to pay for return shipping, MAYBE. See below for details.
Policy does NOT apply to international shipping.
How do I exchange my order?
- Go to Your Orders and select Return or replace items beside the item you want to exchange.
- Select the item you want to exchange, and select a reason from the Reason for return menu.
Warranty and Repairs
  1. Go to Your Orders.
  2. Select Get Product Support next to the eligible item (you'll only see this option if it’s eligible for technical support).
  3. Select one of the following options:
    • Call or chat with live product support
    • Find links to product manuals
    • Contact manufacturer support
    • Review self-help troubleshooting steps or videos.
